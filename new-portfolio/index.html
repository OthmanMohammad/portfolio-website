About Me

I am a dedicated Machine Learning Engineer with extensive experience in developing and deploying machine learning models and pipelines. My work focuses on leveraging state-of-the-art deep learning frameworks and MLOps practices to create scalable and efficient AI solutions. I have a strong background in processing and analyzing large-scale datasets, and I thrive on collaborating with stakeholders to align project requirements with machine learning initiatives.

With a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, I have a solid foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of AI and ML. I have worked with a variety of tools and platforms, including PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras, and Hugging Face Transformers, and I have expertise in optimization techniques such as quantization, LoRA, and distributed training using PyTorch Lightning and DeepSpeed.
